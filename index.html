Formulas

Exit pupil coverage ratio — from your min/max exit pupil (defaults: 0.5mm to 7mm, the eye's typical range):

R = EP_max / EP_min

Step multiplier — from the magnification step % (p, e.g. 40% → 0.40):

k = 1 + p

Number of steps needed to span the ratio R in increments of k:

n = ln(R) / ln(k)

Eyepiece count — steps rounded up, plus one endpoint:

N = ceil( ln(R) / ln(1 + p) ) + 1

Exit pupil at eyepiece i (i = 1 ... N), spaced evenly on a log scale from EP_min to EP_max:

EP_i = EP_min × k^( i × n / (N − 1) )

Focal length of eyepiece i, given telescope focal ratio f/#:

FL_i = EP_i × f/#
