Formula Reference

Percent Step Mode

\[ {\color{#ffe082} N} = \left\lceil \frac{\ln({\color{#ffe082} EP_{\max}} / {\color{#ffe082} EP_{\min}})}{\ln\left(1 + \frac{{\color{#ffe082} Step\%}}{100}\right)} \right\rceil + 1 \]

Where:
• \(N\) is the recommended quantity of eyepieces in your set.
• \(EP_{\max}\) and \(EP_{\min}\) are the maximum and minimum exit pupil inputs.
• \(Step\%\) is the magnification step size percentage.
• \(\lceil \dots \rceil\) denotes the ceiling function (rounding up to the next integer).

Percent Brightness Step Mode

\[ {\color{#ffe082} N} = \left\lceil \frac{\ln({\color{#ffe082} EP_{\max}} / {\color{#ffe082} EP_{\min}})}{\ln\left(\sqrt{1 + \frac{{\color{#ffe082} Step\%}}{100}}\right)} \right\rceil + 1 \]

Where:
• \(N\) is the recommended quantity of eyepieces in your set.
• \(EP_{\max}\) and \(EP_{\min}\) are the maximum and minimum exit pupil inputs.
• \(Step\%\) is the brightness step size percentage.
• \(\lceil \dots \rceil\) denotes the ceiling function (rounding up to the next integer).

Fixed Magnification Step Mode

\[ {\color{#ffe082} N} = \left\lceil \frac{\frac{{\color{#ffe082} F_l}}{{\color{#ffe082} EP_{\min}} \times {\color{#ffe082} F_r}} - \frac{{\color{#ffe082} F_l}}{{\color{#ffe082} EP_{\max}} \times {\color{#ffe082} F_r}}}{{\color{#ffe082} Step}} \right\rceil + 1 \]

Where:
• \(N\) is the recommended quantity of eyepieces in your set.
• \(F_l\) is the telescope focal length.
• \(F_r\) is the telescope focal ratio.
• \(EP_{\max}\) and \(EP_{\min}\) are the maximum and minimum exit pupil inputs.
• \(Step\) is the desired magnification step size (e.g. \(40\text{x}\)).
• \(\lceil \dots \rceil\) denotes the ceiling function (rounding up to the next integer).

Fixed Exit Pupil Step Mode

\[ {\color{#ffe082} N} = \left\lceil \frac{{\color{#ffe082} EP_{\max}} - {\color{#ffe082} EP_{\min}}}{{\color{#ffe082} Step}} \right\rceil + 1 \]

Where:
• \(N\) is the recommended quantity of eyepieces in your set.
• \(EP_{\max}\) and \(EP_{\min}\) are the maximum and minimum exit pupil inputs.
• \(Step\) is the desired exit pupil step size (in mm).
• \(\lceil \dots \rceil\) denotes the ceiling function (rounding up to the next integer).
